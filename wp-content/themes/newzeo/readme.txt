== Usage manual ==
1. Theme option (Appearance > Newzeo Options) : fill twitter with your twitter username without '@' , facebook page url, and rss with your account url.
2. To activate author avatar rich snippet, fill author url with google+ profile url. You can check rich snippet here : http://www.google.com/webmasters/tools/richsnippets
3. To show thumbnail in related post, recent post, please select featured image (post-thumbnail) in post.
